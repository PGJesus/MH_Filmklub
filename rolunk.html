About us

Our film club brings movie lovers together to celebrate the art of cinema. We host weekly screenings of classics, new releases, and hidden gems, followed by lively discussions that dive deeper into film history, storytelling, and production. Members also enjoy workshops, guest speaker sessions, and special events designed to enhance appreciation of film. Whether you’re a casual moviegoer or a passionate cinephile, our club offers a welcoming space to discover, learn, and share the magic of movies.
